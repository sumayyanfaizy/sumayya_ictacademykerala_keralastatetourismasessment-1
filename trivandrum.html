Neyyar Dam & Wildlife Sanctuary


Famous for its Lion and Deer Safari, Neyyar Wildlife Sanctuary is a treat for nature enthusiasts.

Kanakakunnu Palace


Close to the Napier Museum, the Kanakakunnu Palace was built under the rule of Travancore King. Today, the palace is host to plethora of cultural programs that are held on its premises.

Poovar Island


Poovar, a small coastal village along the natural harbour at Vizhinjam, is believed to have been the place called 'Ophir' where the ships of the biblical king Solomon had arrived.
